The Big Story

Johor allocates RM3.2M for Tamil school repairs

New Straits Times — 28 February 2026
The Johor state government has earmarked RM3.2 million specifically for structural repairs at 14 Tamil schools across the state. The allocation, announced during the state assembly budget session, covers roofing, drainage, and electrical work — the three most common complaints from school boards in the state.
Why it matters: This is the first state-level allocation specifically earmarked for Tamil schools since 2019. If Johor delivers, it sets a precedent other states will find hard to ignore.
