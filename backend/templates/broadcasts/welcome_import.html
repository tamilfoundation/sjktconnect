Introducing SJK(T) Connect

You are receiving this because you are, or have been, a valued donor or member of the Malaysian Tamil Educational Research & Development Foundation (Tamil Foundation).

We are excited to share our newest initiative — SJK(T) Connect (tamilschool.org), a platform that monitors and analyses everything happening around Malaysia’s 528 Tamil schools.

What you’ll receive

News Watch — fortnightly digest of Tamil school news from major media
Urgent Alerts — time-sensitive developments that need community attention
Monthly Intelligence Blast — trends, patterns, and opportunities across all 528 schools

Every email includes real data from Parliament Hansard records, news sources, and school-level statistics — so our community stays informed and can act when it matters.

This is not spam. You can manage your preferences or unsubscribe at any time using the links at the bottom of every email.
