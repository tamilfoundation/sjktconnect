A platform that monitors and analyses everything happening around Malaysia's 528 Tamil schools — from policy announcements to local initiatives, school news to parliamentary mentions.

What you'll receive

News Watch — Fortnightly digest of Tamil school news and developments
Urgent Alerts — Time-sensitive developments that impact Tamil schools
